Promoting a sustainable environment

ShoppersBag is a digital technology that drives the reduction in production, usage, and disposal of single-use plastics in the retail value chain. Our mission is to support shoppers to maintain a sustainability lifestyle and improve the livelihoods of women and girls.

A growth engine for plastic
reduction, reuse and recycling

We created the ShoppersBag App for shoppers to earn rewards on every usage of reusable shopping bags in stores and malls - working with Ecofriendly Bag Producers, Brands, Stores and Malls to drive sustainability in Africa's Retail Value Chain.

The adoption of ShoppersBag to increase the usage of alternative reusable, biodegradable, and recyclable shopping bags; will support shoppers to maintain a sustainable lifestyle aimed at reducing the number of single-use plastic bags in circulation, and promote a sustainable environment that empowers women and girls to improve their livelihoods.

As eco-conscious individuals and business owners ourselves, we draw inspiration from a vision of millions of exceptional shoppers across the continent who desire to shop all their needs, earn from simple tasks, while saving the planet. We believe that on our journey, shoppers in Africa will stay loyal to the best brands on the planet, who are committed to driving Sustainability in the Retail Value Chain. Building that sustainable society, free from plastic and single-use plastics pollution.
